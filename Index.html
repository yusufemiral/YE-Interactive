Privacy Policy

Oops Run mobile game is developed by YE Interactive (“we”, “our”, or “us”). This page is used to inform visitors regarding our policies with the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information for anyone using our Service.

1. Information Collection

Oops Run does not directly collect any personally identifiable information. However, third-party advertising and analytics providers integrated in the game may collect certain technical and usage data, such as device identifiers, advertising IDs, and usage statistics.

2. Use of Information

The collected data may be used for the following purposes:

  • Displaying and optimizing advertisements
  • Analyzing game performance (e.g., via Firebase Analytics)
  • Detecting errors and improving user experience

3. Third-Party Services

Oops Run may use the following third-party services for advertising and analytics:

  • Google (AdMob & Firebase): https://policies.google.com/privacy
  • AppLovin: https://www.applovin.com/privacy/
  • Unity Ads & ironSource: https://unity.com/legal/privacy-policy
  • Mintegral: https://www.mintegral.com/en/privacy
  • Meta Audience Network: https://www.facebook.com/about/privacy/
  • Pangle: https://www.pangleglobal.com/privacy
  • Liftoff: https://liftoff.io/privacy-policy/

These third-party services operate under their own privacy policies. We encourage you to review them carefully.

4. Data Storage and Security

We do not store any personal data ourselves. Any data collected by third-party services is stored and secured under their own privacy and security practices. YE Interactive does not have direct access to this data.

5. Your Choices and Opt-Out

You can control or opt-out of personalized advertising in the game by adjusting your mobile device's settings:

  • On iOS: Go to Settings > Privacy & Security > Advertising and turn on "Limit Ad Tracking."
  • On Android: Go to Settings > Google > Ads and tap "Opt-out of Ads Personalization" or "Delete Advertising ID."

For data collected by our third-party partners (listed in Section 3), please refer to their respective privacy policies (linked above) to understand and exercise your rights regarding data collection and usage.

6. Children’s Privacy

These Services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we take steps to delete this information from our servers immediately.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, you are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
