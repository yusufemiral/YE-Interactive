Privacy Policy

Oops Run — by YE Interactive

Effective Date: March 27, 2026
1

Information Collection

Oops Run does not directly collect any personally identifiable information. However, third-party advertising and analytics providers integrated in the game may collect certain technical and usage data, such as:

  • Device identifiers and advertising IDs
  • Usage statistics and session data
  • App interaction and performance metrics
2

Use of Information

The data collected by third-party services may be used for the following purposes:

  • Displaying and optimizing advertisements
  • Analyzing game performance and usage patterns
  • Remote configuration of ad placements and game settings
  • Detecting errors and improving user experience

Data Storage and Security

5

Your Choices and Opt-Out

You can control or opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your Android device settings:

Android
Go to Settings → Google → Ads and tap "Opt-out of Ads Personalization" or "Delete Advertising ID".

For data collected by our third-party partners (listed in Section 3), please refer to their respective privacy policies to understand and exercise your rights regarding data collection and usage.

6

Children's Privacy

This service does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

If you are a parent or guardian and you are aware that your child has provided personal information, please contact us. If we discover that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete this information immediately.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page with an updated effective date.
